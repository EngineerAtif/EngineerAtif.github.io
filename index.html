CFD Analysis

Computational fluid dynamics simulations that reveal flow behaviour, pressure drops, and thermal performance — before a single prototype is cut.

FEA & Structural

Finite element analysis for static loads, fatigue life, vibration modes, and impact events. Designs proven virtually before they are proven physically.

Production-grade mold designs with optimised gate locations, draft angles, sink-mark prevention, and complete tool-making packages ready for the shop floor.

3D Scanning · Point Cloud · IGES/STEP

SolidWorks Motion · Adams · MBD

ANSYS Thermal · Simulation Studio
